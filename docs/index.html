1 · Which packs have the best odds?

The headline finding: Courtyard tunes every pack to a ~97% expected value — for each $100 spent, the average item pulled comps at ~$97. Since EV is flat across the board, "best odds" comes down to your chance of pulling an item worth at least what you paid (the hit rate), and the jackpot ceiling you're chasing.

2 · How each collection moved over the last year

Each chart tracks a market index for that collection, rebased to 100 in July 2025. Chips show the change over the last 3, 6 and 12 months. These are category-level market indices from the sources cited on each card — not Courtyard-specific prices.

Methodology. Pack odds: pulled from Courtyard's public pack API (api.courtyard.io/vending-machines) — the same data behind the "Odds" dropdown on each pack page. Hit rate is the summed probability of odds buckets whose minimum value ≥ pack price. Collection price action: publicly reported market indices and sales data as cited per card; where a source publishes only period returns, the monthly path between anchor points is interpolated (marked "est." on the card).
